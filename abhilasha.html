About Abhilasha




“Design is intelligence made visible" — Alina Wheeler

Students, welcome to the Design Launchpad!

Abhilasha is a design launchpad for young curious minds. We bring you an opportunity to be a part of a full day experience in the field of design and get to know how design is visible intelligence. Look, hear and learn from the spear-headers of design in the Northeast. Be involved in a hands-on skill based workshop to see your ideas come live.

Lastly, get inspired from some of the innovative ideas by designers!

Date: 21st May, 2019
Time: 9am to 5pm
Venue: Dr. Bhupen Hazarika Auditorium, IIT Guwahati
For Students From Class 8 - 12

 


Expert message(s)


"In my 11th standard, everyone was telling me what I should do - I really didn't know what I wanted to do"

'Abhilasha' is a workshop for Schools that will open your mind to the creative and innovative abilities in you.

There will be enjoyable 'creative do-it-yourself' workshops offered by experienced faculty of the Department of Design. Presentations will inform you of career opportunities in the field of Design - Graphic Design, Product Design, New Media and Interaction Design.

You can get information on the leading schools of Design in India. Exhibitions of Design students projects will inform you of the Design education program at IIT Guwahati.

Come join us for a day of 'learning by doing'.

Ravi Mokashi Punekar
Professor - DoD, IIT Guwahati

Events

Abhilasha will have the following flagship events

Lecture

  • Faculty members of DoD IITG shall introduce the students to the stories of design & their opportunities

Workshop

  • All students shall be part of a hands on material based workshop based on a design brief. The material shall be provided.

Showcase

  • Students shall display their creativity in an open forum. Participants will be awarded certificates and goodies.
